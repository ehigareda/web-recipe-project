Fish Tacos

Description:

Fish tacos, tacos of the fish variety, gonna taste really good. If it doesn't taste good, you definately just made it wrong, stick to grilled cheeses buddy.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Tilapia
  • Cilantro
  • Guacamole
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Season the tilapia filets with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  2. Place the tilapia in an oiled skillet on medium-high heat for 5 minutes on each side
  3. Heat up tortillas on a pan
  4. Break the tilapia up with a fork, assemble taco
  5. EAT
